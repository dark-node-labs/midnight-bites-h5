About

About Bitesize Arcade

Bitesize Arcade is a small home for free online mini games that open quickly, work in a browser, and fit into short breaks. The site focuses on lightweight arcade loops: clear goals, simple controls, readable screens, and quick replay value.

Featured Game

Midnight Bites

Serve street-food orders, keep your combo alive, and play a quick restaurant game without downloading an app.

What We Publish

Bitesize Arcade publishes browser games that are designed for mobile and desktop play. Current games include restaurant, food, snack, and time management challenges, with more mini games planned as the arcade grows.

Our Design Goals

Each game should be easy to start, understandable without a long tutorial, and replayable in short sessions. The site avoids downloads, account gates, and complicated setup so players can move from a page to a playable game quickly.

Site Information

Independent Browser Games

Bitesize Arcade is independently maintained. The games are original web experiences built for casual entertainment, not gambling, real-money rewards, downloads, or account-based competition. Scores and preferences may be stored locally in the player's browser when a game needs settings such as sound, city selection, or best score memory.

The editorial pages around each game explain how the games work, what kind of player they may suit, and how they fit into related categories such as food games, restaurant games, and time management games.
