Snack Stack Sprint

Handle three snack tickets at once, grab the next ingredient from a moving belt, and keep customers patient through the rush. Choose the right order, chain clean service, and trigger Focus Time when the belt gets too fast.

How to Play

Click one customer ticket, then click the matching next ingredient on the conveyor belt. Completing a ticket scores points and brings in a new order. Wrong ingredients or slow service reduce patience. When the Focus meter fills, use it to slow the belt for a short recovery window.
