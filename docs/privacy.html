Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Midnight Bites is a static casual web game. The current version does not require account registration and does not actively collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, or other direct identity information.

Local Storage

The game may use browser local storage to save city selection, best scores, sound settings, and similar preferences. This data stays in your browser and can be removed by clearing site data.

Analytics and Third Parties

The current version uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate website traffic, page views, device types, and similar usage patterns. The site does not require account registration and does not send names, phone numbers, email addresses, game scores, or direct identity information to Google Analytics. The current version does not include ads, login SDKs, or payment SDKs.

Children

The game is recommended for ages 8 and up. Children should play with permission and guidance from a parent or guardian and should avoid long continuous sessions.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the maintainer through the project page or repository issues.
