Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

Bitesize Arcade is a collection of static casual browser games. The site does not require account registration and does not actively collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, payment details, or other direct identity information from players.

Local Storage

The game may use browser local storage to save city selection, best scores, sound settings, and similar preferences. This data stays in your browser and can be removed by clearing site data.

Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics 4 to understand aggregate website traffic, page views, device types, and similar usage patterns. Bitesize Arcade does not send names, phone numbers, email addresses, payment information, or individual game scores to Google Analytics.

Advertising Cookies

Bitesize Arcade may display advertising through Google AdSense or related Google advertising services. Third party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to serve ads to users based on visits to Bitesize Arcade and other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Users may also learn more about third-party advertising opt-out options at aboutads.info.

If additional third-party ad vendors or ad networks are used in the future, they may also use cookies or similar technologies for ad delivery, frequency capping, measurement, or personalization where allowed by law.

Children

The games are intended for general casual entertainment and are recommended for players ages 8 and up with parent or guardian guidance where appropriate. Bitesize Arcade does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian believes a child has provided personal information, please contact us so it can be reviewed and removed where applicable.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Bitesize Arcade at hello@bitesizearcade.com.
